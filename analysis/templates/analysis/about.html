What Is Bayan?

Bayan is an API developed using AI and NLP technologies to analyze customer opinions in Salla stores. It aims to provide merchants with information about their store based on written customer comments, to improve the store and enhance customer satisfaction.

Where did Bayan start?

The idea of Bayan started at Umm Al-Qura University as a university graduation project, as a group we decided to do this website and named "Bayan" and it was implemented to support e-commerce and the ongoing digital transformation witnessed by the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Our Vision

Empowering business owners in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia to gain insights into the written feelings and preferences of their customers to make sound and data-driven decisions to improve customer experiences. We aim to bridge the gap between companies and their customers, which makes the customer realize that his opinion is heard and important.

Why choose Bayan?

In today's business world, where competition is increasing and customer satisfaction is a key factor for success, Bayan emerges as a smart solution to help to understand and analyze customers' feedback accurately and efficiently. Whether you are an e-commerce store owner, product manager, or customer experience specialist, Bayan is your ideal partner to understand your customers and enhance your services based on real insights.

Bayan Features

Advanced Review and Feedback Analysis

Extract valuable insights from customer comments to understand their needs and improve your services.

Powerful AI Technology

Bayan utilizes the most suitable AI techniques for sentiment analysis and review classification.

Time and Effort Saving

No need for manual comment analysis, simply upload your feedback file, and Bayan will handle the rest.

Actionable Suggestions and Recommendations

Receive clear insights and recommendations to help you make strategic, data-driven decisions.

Arabic Language Support

A sophisticated system capable of understanding different dialects and processing natural language with high accuracy.
